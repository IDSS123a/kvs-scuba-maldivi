## Getting Access

### For New Members

If you're not yet registered for the expedition, follow these steps to request access:

#### Step 1: Visit the App
- Open the KVS-SCUBA Maldives 2026 expedition website/app
- You will see a form titled **"Request Access"**

#### Step 2: Fill Out the Form
Complete all fields with your information:
- **Full Name** - Your complete name (e.g., "Davor Mulaliƒá")
- **Email Address** - A valid email you check regularly
- **Phone Number** - Your phone number (with country code, e.g., +387 61 234 5678)
- **SSI Diver Number** - Your SSI certification number (or leave blank if unsure)

**Important:** Make sure your email and phone number are correct! The organizers will use these to contact you.

#### Step 3: Submit Your Request
- Click the **"Submit Request"** button
- You will see a confirmation message: **"Your Request Was Sent!"**
- You may now close the form

#### Step 4: Wait for Approval
- The expedition organizers (Davor, Adnan, or Samir) will review your request
- They will contact you via email or phone with your **6-digit Access PIN**
- **Keep this PIN safe!** You'll need it to log in

#### What Happens Next?
Once approved, you will receive a message with:
- Your personal 6-digit PIN code
- A link to this app
- Instructions to log in

## Logging In

### When You Have Your PIN

#### Step 1: Open the App
- Visit the expedition portal URL
- You will see a screen with the heading **"Unlock Expedition"**
- There will be a PIN input field

#### Step 2: Enter Your PIN
- The field displays: **"Enter your 6-digit access PIN"**
- Type your PIN carefully (numbers only, no spaces)
- Example: `123456`
- You must enter all 6 digits to proceed

#### Step 3: Click "Unlock"
- Once you've entered 6 digits, the **"Unlock"** button becomes active
- Click the button
- The system verifies your PIN

#### Step 4: Success!
- If your PIN is correct, you'll be logged in immediately
- You'll be directed to the expedition dashboard
- Your name appears at the top of the page

### PIN Login Tips

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| **Only Digits** | The PIN accepts only numbers (0-9). Letters and symbols are ignored. |
| **6 Digits** | Your PIN is always 6 digits long. |
| **Case Sensitive?** | No - only numbers, so not applicable. |
| **Case Sensitive (Device)** | No - your device's caps lock doesn't matter. |
| **Spaces** | Don't include spaces. Just type: `123456` not `123 456` |

### What if You Forget Your PIN?

**Don't Worry!** Follow these steps:

1. Click the **"Request Access"** link on the login screen
2. Fill out the form again (same as the initial request)
3. In the message or contact the organizers directly
4. Tell them you need a **new PIN** (your existing one can be regenerated)
5. They will generate a new PIN and send it to you

### Too Many Wrong Attempts?

If you enter an incorrect PIN 5 times in a row:
- You will see an error: **"Too many attempts. Try again in 5 minutes."**
- **Your account is temporarily locked** for security reasons
- Wait 5 minutes before trying again
- If your PIN is correct, you'll be able to log in

## Using the App

### After You Log In

Once logged in, you'll see the main **Expedition Dashboard** with several sections:

#### Dashboard Overview
- **Countdown Timer** - Days until the expedition starts (January 5, 2026)
- **Weather** - Current conditions in Maldives
- **Essential Information** - Details about the Maldives, what to pack, cultural info

#### Main Navigation Menu
At the top or side of the screen, you'll see buttons/tabs for:

| Section | What You Can Do |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Itinerary** | See the day-by-day expedition schedule. Click on dive sites to learn more. |
| **Participants** | View photos and info of other expedition members. |
| **Gallery** | Upload and view photos from the trip. (Available after trip starts) |
| **Preparation** | Personal checklist - what to bring, what to prepare. |
| **Informacije** / **Information** | Bilingual expedition details and guides. |

### Example: Viewing the Itinerary

1. Click **"Itinerary"** from the main menu
2. You'll see a map of the Maldives with dive sites marked
3. Click on any site to see:
   - Dive difficulty (Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced)
   - Expected depth and visibility
   - Marine life you might see
   - Specific activities planned for that day

### Example: Uploading a Photo

1. Click **"Gallery"** from the main menu
2. Click **"Upload Photo"** button
3. Choose a photo from your device
4. Select a category: Dive, Group, Meal, Fun, Other
5. Add a caption (optional)
6. Click **"Upload"**
7. Your photo appears in the gallery immediately

### Bilingual Support

- Look for a language toggle (usually top-right)
- Click to switch between languages
- All text in the app updates instantly

## Troubleshooting

### "Invalid PIN or access not approved"

**What it means:** Your PIN is incorrect, or your access hasn't been approved yet.

**What to do:**
1. Double-check that you entered the PIN correctly (no extra spaces or characters)
2. Make sure your account is approved (you should have received a message from the organizers)
3. If you think your PIN is wrong, contact one of the organizers
4. They can generate a new PIN for you

### "Too many attempts. Try again in 5 minutes."

**What it means:** You've tried to log in with an incorrect PIN 5 times. Your account is temporarily locked for security.

**What to do:**
1. Wait 5 minutes
2. Try again with the correct PIN
3. If you still can't remember it, contact an organizer

### "Your account was revoked"

**What it means:** An administrator has disabled your access (unusual - this shouldn't happen unless there's an issue).

**What to do:**
- Contact one of the organizers immediately
- They can re-approve your account

### Page Won't Load / "Error Loading Data"

**What it means:** There may be a temporary connection problem or server issue.

**What to do:**
1. Refresh the page (press F5 or click the browser refresh button)
2. Check your internet connection
3. Try again in a few minutes
4. If it persists, contact an organizer

### I'm Logged Out Unexpectedly

**What it means:** Your session expired or your browser cleared its memory.

**What to do:**
1. Log back in with your PIN
2. Your data is safe - nothing was lost
3. If this happens frequently, clear your browser cache and try again
